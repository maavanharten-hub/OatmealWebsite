Pumpkin Oatmeal

Cozy fall oats with pumpkin puree, maple syrup and nuts.

Oatmeal for dinner

I love pumpkin and I was craving oatmeal for dinner in the fall. So I made a pumpkin puree and topped my oatmeal with all kinds of delicious toppings, including a high protein chocolate cream. It was honestly amazing.

Ingredients

  • 100g pumpkin
  • 50g oats
  • tsp cinnamon
  • 200ml soy milk + 15ml
  • 15g chocolate protein powder
  • 15g soy yogurt
  • tsp peanut butter
  • tsp maple syrup
  • Chopped nuts
  • optional: Roasted pumpkin seeds

Preparation

  1. Dice the pumpkin and boil in water until soft, approximately 10 minutes. Save some of the cooking water.
  2. Mash to a fine puree, use some of the cooking water to make it scoopable.
  3. Add pumpkin puree, oats, cinnamon and 200ml soy milk to a pan, heat and let it bubble until desired consistency, about 10 minutes.
  4. Make protein chocolate cream: mix protein powder, 15ml soy milk and soy yoghurt until creamy.
  5. Put the oatmeal in a bowl and top with the chocolate cream, peanut butter, maple syrup, nuts and pumpkin seeds.
