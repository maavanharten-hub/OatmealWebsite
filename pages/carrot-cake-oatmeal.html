Carrot Cake Oatmeal

I love carrot cake and what is better than starting the morning with some cake in a bowl? It adds some vitamins as well. I topped mine with strawberry, peanut butter and a protein vanilla cream.

Celebration in a bowl!

Yes, you can have breakfast that tastes just like a cake! Perfect with a nice cup of coffee or tea. In this recipe I added strawberries and (of course) peanut butter, but you can go wild! Raisins and nuts work really well, as well as banana.

Ingredients

  • 50g oats
  • 50g carrots
  • cinnamon, nutmeg, ground ginger
  • splash vanilla extract
  • 250ml soy milk + 15ml
  • 15g soy yogurt
  • tsp peanut butter
  • tsp maple syrup
  • fresh fruit, I used strawberries but banana also works well!
  • optional: raisins, walnuts or pecan nuts, seeds

Preparation

  1. Grate the carrot and put in a pan with oats, spices, vanilla and 250ml soy milk.
  2. Heat on medium heat and when it starts bubbling, turn it to low and let simmer until desired consistency, approximately 10 minutes.
  3. Mix protein powder with 15ml soy milk and soy yogurt.
  4. Add carrot oatmeal to a bowl, top with the protein cream, maple syrup, peanut butter and other toppings if desired.
