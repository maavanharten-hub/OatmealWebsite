Zucchini Oatmeal

I like to add some volume and vitamins by grating zucchini into my oatmeal before cooking. I promise you won't taste it! In this case I used my favourite toppings.

Volume & Vitamins

Grating zucchini into your oatmeal adds to the volume so you can enjoy your oatmeal for just a little bit longer. Added bonus is that it also adds some vitamins with your first meal in the morning. And don't worry about it becoming a savoury dish, it will taste just the same with all your favourite toppings. In this recipe I use one of my favourite topping combinations, but you can literally use anything.

Ingredients

  • 50g zucchini
  • 50g oats
  • tsp cinnamon
  • 200ml soy milk + 15ml
  • 15g chocolate protein powder
  • 15g soy yogurt
  • tsp peanut butter
  • half a banana
  • piece of dark chocolate

Preparation

  1. Grate the zucchini.
  2. Add oats, grated zucchini, cinnamon and 200ml soy milk to a pan, heat and let it bubble until desired consistency, about 10 minutes.
  3. Make protein chocolate cream: mix protein powder, 15ml soy milk and soy yoghurt until creamy.
  4. Cut the dark chocolate in small pieces.
  5. Put the oatmeal in a bowl and top with the chocolate cream, peanut butter, banana and chocolate.
