Chocolate Peanut Oatmeal

Brownie vibes in the morning with this chocolate oatmeal with blueberries, peanuts and peanut butter.

Snickers in a bowl

Chocolate and peanuts are high on my list of ultimate favourite combos. I like to add vegan chocolate protein powder, because it gives the oats a sweet, chocolatey flavour and also adds some protein. Of course these toppings are a suggestion; you can go wild! Banana or strawberries would be a great alternative to the blueberries, and feel free to add some dark chocolate, other nuts or seeds, etc...

Ingredients

  • 50g oats
  • 10g cocoa powder
  • 250ml soy milk
  • 15g chocolate protein powder
  • tsp peanut butter
  • (salted) peanuts
  • optional: fresh fruit, I used blueberries but banana or strawberries also works well!
  • optional: dark chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Heat oats, cocoa powder and soy milk in a pan on low to medium heat and let it bubble for approximately 10 minutes.
  2. Take the pan off the heat when it is still a bit more liquid than desired, then mix in the protein powder.
  3. Add peanut butter, fruit and other toppings if desired!
