Vanilla Blueberry Oatmeal

Can't go wrong with a nice bowl of vanilla oats with cinnamon, topped with blueberries, banana, seeds and peanut butter.

Classic vanilla

Vanilla is such a classic flavour but it never fails to warm my heart. It's pure coziness in a bowl. Of course with my favourite peanut butter and banana is also a staple in my oatmeal bowls.

Ingredients

  • 50g oats
  • tsp cinnamon
  • 250ml soy milk
  • splash of vanilla extract
  • 15g vanilla protein powder
  • handful of fresh blueberries
  • half a banana
  • tsp peanut butter
  • tsp flax seeds

Preparation

  1. Heat oats, vanilla extract, cinnamon and soy milk in a pan on low to medium heat and let it bubble for approximately 10 minutes until it is just a bit more liquid than desired.
  2. Mix in the vanilla protein powder.
  3. Slice the banana.
  4. Put the oatmeal in a bowl and top with the blueberries, banana, peanut butter and flax seeds.
