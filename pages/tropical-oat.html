Tropical Oatmeal

Summer vibes with vanilla oatmeal with lots of mango, vanilla protein yogurt and dark chocolate.

Mmmango!

Sometimes I am really craving mango and it always feels like a special, summery treat. I decided to add it to my bowl of oatmeal and it was a hit! I can imagine it also works really well in combination with coconut.

Ingredients

  • 50g oats
  • splash of vanilla
  • 250ml soy milk OR 125ml coconut milk + 125ml water
  • 15ml soy milk
  • 15g soy yogurt
  • 15g vanilla protein powder
  • 100g fresh mango
  • tsp peanut butter
  • piece of dark chocolate
  • optional: coconut flakes, chia seeds

    • Preparation

    1. Heat oats, vanilla and soymilk or coconut milk + water in a pan on medium heat and let it bubble on low heat until desired consistency, about 10 minutes.
    2. Meanwhile, mix 15ml soy milk, soy yoghurt and vanilla protein powder to a smooth paste.
    3. Cut the mango in cubes and cut the chocolate in small pieces.
    4. Put oatmeal in a bowl and top with the vanilla protein paste, mango, peanut butter and chocolate.
    5. Add the other optional toppings as well or go wild with your own ideas.
