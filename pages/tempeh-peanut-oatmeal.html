Tempeh Peanut Oatmeal

My weirdest creation yet. I was craving oatmeal for dinner and added roasted tempeh, maple syrup and peanuts to my bowl. It was actually really satisfying..

A hearty dinner

I am going through a tempeh phase and the oatmeal game is also still going strong. It may seem like a weird combo but it was really easy to make this for dinner when I didn't feel like cooking and it hit all the right spots.

Ingredients

  • 50g oats
  • 75g tempeh
  • tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp maple syrup
  • 50g grated zucchini
  • 100ml soy milk
  • 100ml water
  • pinch salt
  • tsp peanut butter
  • salted peanuts
  • optional: crispy chili oil or sriracha

    • Preparation

    1. Cube the tempeh and toss with soy sauce and 1tsp of maple syrup.
    2. Roast in airfryer (10 minutes) or oven (20 minutes) at 200ºC.
    3. Meanwhile, heat the oats with grated zucchini, water, soy milk and a pinch of salt on medium heat, when it almost boils, let it simmer until desired consistency (approx. 10 min).
    4. Put the oatmeal in a bowl and top with peanut butter, a tsp of maple syrup, tempeh and peanuts.
    5. Add some crispy chili oil or sriracha if you want to add a little spice!
