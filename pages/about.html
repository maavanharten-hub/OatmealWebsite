Hi there! My name is Marieke and welcome to my oatmeal website.

I learned to eat oatmeal for the first time when I had reached adulthood, but I am hooked ever since and have been eating it consistently for 10+ years.

My love for oats

My parents were not a fan of oatmeal, so when I was younger I never had it at home and thought I wouldn't like it either. But when I was 18 I started living on my own and saw all these good-looking bowls on social media. I thought I'd give it a try. I am now 30 and still look forward to eating my oatmeal every day.

Oatmeal variations

During these years, I went to multiple phases of oats. From plain oatmeal to heavily loaded bowls and additional protein by adding protein powder, from water to soy milk, from preparing in the microwave to stovetop oats. I have tried a lot and I definitely have my favourite combos. I am also vegan so all of my recipes are without use of animal ingredients.
