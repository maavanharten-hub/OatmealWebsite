Espressoats

Give your day a boost with espresso-infused oats! I like mine with fruit, peanut butter and in this case, crumbled Speculoos cookies.

Coffee and oats in one

Some mornings you just need an extra boost to start your day. Usually I just have oats and coffee, but what if you combine the two? The addition of cocoa gives it a nice mocha flavour that works really well with fresh fruit and the sweetness of the cookie. I add chocolate protein powder as well, but you can also add a bit of sweetener of choice to make it a bit sweeter!

Ingredients

  • 50g oats
  • 10g cocoa powder
  • 200ml soy milk
  • Freshly brewed espresso or strong coffee
  • Optional: 15g chocolate protein powder
  • OptionalL tsp of maple syrup or other sweetener
  • Biscoff speculoos cookie
  • optional: tsp peanut butter
  • Fresh fruit, I used blueberries and strawberries
  • optional: dark chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Heat oats, cocoa powder, espresso and soy milk in a pan on low to medium heat and let it bubble for approximately 10 minutes until it is still a little bit more liquid than desired.
  2. Add in the protein powder.
  3. Finish with peanut butter, fresh fruit, a crumbled cookie and optionally sweetener or dark chocolate!
